## Response Time Simulation

The chatbot simulates natural typing delay:

- **Minimum**: 1 second
- **Maximum**: 2 seconds
- **Random**: Between 1-2 seconds for realism

During this time:
- Input field is disabled
- Send button is disabled
- Typing indicator shows (three animated dots)
