### Review Generation

**Strategy:**
- Ask satisfied customers to review on Google
- Offer incentive (entry into monthly draw)
- Make it easy: provide direct link to Google Business
- Respond to ALL reviews (positive & negative)

**Sample Review Request Message:**
```
"Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for choosing Club Caddy Carts for your [event/purchase]!

We'd love to hear about your experience. Could you share a brief review on Google?
It only takes 30 seconds and helps other Aucklanders discover quality golf cart solutions.

[Click to review us]

Cheers,
Club Caddy Carts Team
```

**Review Response Examples:**

For Positive Reviews:
```
"Thank you so much [Name]! We're thrilled you had a great experience with our team.
Your feedback means everything to us. We look forward to serving you again soon!

- Club Caddy Carts"
```

For Negative Reviews:
```
"Hi [Name],

Thank you for your feedback. We're sorry to hear [specific issue]. This isn't the standard
we strive for, and we'd like to make it right. Please contact us directly at [phone]
so we can resolve this properly.

- Club Caddy Carts"
```

## 7. Schema Markup Implementation

### Required Schema (JSON-LD)

Add to your site's `<head>`:

```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Club Caddy Carts",
  "image": "https://clubcaddycarts.com/logo.png",
  "description": "Premium electric golf cart rental and sales in Auckland, New Zealand",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "streetAddress": "[Your Address]",
    "addressLocality": "Auckland",
    "postalCode": "[Postcode]",
    "addressCountry": "NZ"
  },
  "telephone": "+64 [phone]",
  "url": "https://clubcaddycarts.com",
  "sameAs": [
    "https://www.facebook.com/clubcaddycarts",
    "https://www.instagram.com/clubcaddycarts"
  ],
  "priceRange": "$$",
  "openingHoursSpecification": [
    {
      "@type": "OpeningHoursSpecification",
      "dayOfWeek": "Monday-Friday",
      "opens": "09:00",
      "closes": "17:00"
    },
    {
      "@type": "OpeningHoursSpecification",
      "dayOfWeek": "Saturday-Sunday",
      "opens": "10:00",
      "closes": "16:00"
    }
  ]
}
```

```json
  }
}
```

## 13. NZ-Specific Considerations

### GST (Goods and Services Tax)
- Ensure prices clearly show GST treatment (15% in NZ)
- Important for business customers

### Legal Compliance
- Road transport rules for golf carts
- Health & Safety at Work Act 2015
- Consumer Guarantees Act 1993
- Fair Trading Act 1986

### Seasonal Trends
- Peak season: October-April (summer/events)
- Off-peak: May-September
- Plan content and promotions around seasons

### Cultural Factors
- Celebrate NZ-specific holidays (Anzac Day, Queen's Birthday, etc.)
- Use Aotearoa/NZ terminology
- Feature diverse customer bases
- Show community involvement
